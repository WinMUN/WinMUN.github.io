Mission Statement

Inspiring diplomacy is the main aim of UWindsor Model United Nations. We are dedicated to providing a diverse environment where students will be inspired to confront the challenging issues that impact ours society today. Our goal is to encourage our members towards new ways of thinking. The WINMUN experience grants students unique and intriguing leadership and educational opportunities as they master the art of diplomacy and prepare for various MUN Conferences across North America.
